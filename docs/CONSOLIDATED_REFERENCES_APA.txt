APA 7TH EDITION FORMATTING NOTES:
=====================================

1. ALPHABETICAL ORDER: References ordered by first author's last name

2. BOOK TITLES: Italicized with sentence case capitalization
   Example: *Software engineering* (11th ed.).

3. JOURNAL ARTICLES: Journal name and volume italicized
   Example: *Journal of Biomedical Informatics, 138*, 104-118.

4. DOIs: Include when available
   Format: https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx

5. WEBSITES: Title italicized, URL included
   Example: *ChatGPT and HIPAA compliance*. https://www...

6. EDITION NUMBERS: In parentheses after title
   Example: (4th ed.)

7. ARTICLE NUMBERS: For journals using article numbers instead of page ranges
   Example: Article 252

8. HANGING INDENT: Second and subsequent lines indented 0.5 inches

9. CAPITALIZATION: 
   - Book/article titles: Sentence case (only first word and proper nouns)
   - Journal names: Title case (all major words capitalized)

10. AUTHORS: Up to 20 authors listed; use "&" before last author
